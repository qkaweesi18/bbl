Helpful tips

Wash & Treat Regularly

Keep the scalp clean and nourished — use a gentle shampoo and follow with a deep conditioning treatment every 2–4 weeks depending on your routine.

  • Pre-poo before washing to reduce breakage
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo
  • Finish with a moisturizing conditioner

Moisturize & Seal

Maintain moisture by using a light leave-in followed by a natural oil to seal. Focus on the ends and any dry areas.

  • Use water-based moisturizers
  • Seal with oils like castor or coconut
  • Protect at night with a satin scarf

Protect Your Edges

Avoid overly tight styles and harsh brushing near the hairline. When installing braids, ask for gentle tension and regular maintenance.

  • Ask for looser parts near the edges
  • Use edge control sparingly
  • Schedule touch-ups for longevity
